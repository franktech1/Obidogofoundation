About Us

Obidogo Foundation: Empowering Tomorrow Leaders Today.

Obidogo Foundation is a dynamic non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders within the Bende, Abia, and across Nigeria.

Founded by Chief Iheanyi Frank Chinasa, Our misssion is to equip young people with the skills and mindset needed to become self-reliant tech-savvy, and committed to buildinga better socity.

At Obidogo Foundation, We believe in the power of education and innovation.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we provide accessto cutting-edge technology training, leadership development, and entrepreneurship support.

Our goal is to inspire and empower the youth to harness their pontential, embrace technology, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Join us as we shape the future of Nigeria by raising leaders who are not only prepared for the challenges of tomorrow but are also passionate about making a positive impact today.

Together, we can build a brighter, more prosperous future for all.
